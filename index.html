❦ We chose a "different" Goan experience ❦

Mercure Devaaya Resort, Divar Island

  • Located on the serene "Island of Love" - Divar Island
  • We loved the unique Indo-Portuguese style architecture
  • Stunning waterfront location on the Mandovi River (Rumors are we can see the otters chilling in the season)
  • Away from traffic and pollution - perfect peaceful getaway
  • 5 minutes from historic Old Goa churches
  • Beautiful landscaped gardens and riverfront venues
  • We really like the local cuisine, in particular their Ros omelette and Chicken Cafreal.
